About us

Unnati Educational and Charitable Trust

Unnati Educational and Charitable Trust is one such organization which is spreading the light of knowledge by removing the darkness of ignorance from the lives of the students. Unnati Educational and Charitable Trust is the task of taking many students out of the bleak future and putting them in the lap of light. Our aim is also to bring light into the lives of the students.

Our Aim

The aim of Unnati Educational and Charitable Trust is to motivate the financially weak and needy students of the society to get higher education and to divert them towards higher education by taking responsibility of their 100% college fees.

100% of Your College Fees, Covered by Us

All tuition fees for students from India and Nepal will be fully borne by the Unnati Educational and Charitable Trust. However, students are required to cover their own accommodation and examination expenses, which are kept at a nominal level.
