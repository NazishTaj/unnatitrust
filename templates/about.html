About us

Unnati Educational and Charitable Trust

Unnati Educational and Charitable Trust is an organization dedicated to spreading the light of education and removing the darkness of ignorance from students’ lives. The trust works with the mission of guiding students away from uncertainty and towards a brighter and more secure future.

The trust operates with the support of Rosemine Educational Trust and is committed to ensuring that financial difficulties do not become a barrier to education. The chairman strongly believes that even if resources are limited, education should never be abandoned.

Chairman Mr. Samar Tanweer is dedicated to supporting students who are forced to discontinue their studies due to high college fees. His vision is to stand by such students and help them complete their education with dignity.

Our Objective

The primary objective of the Unnati Educational and Charitable Trust is to encourage financially weak and deserving students to pursue higher education. The trust supports such students by taking full responsibility for their college tuition fees.

The trust aims to improve the country’s Gross Enrollment Ratio by helping needy students enroll in higher education. Students with at least 50% marks in Class 10 or 12 can apply.

The financial support provided by the trust is a scholarship, not a loan, and students are not required to repay the college fees.

⚠ Attention ! Students of India and Nepal

Students from India and Nepal can apply for admission to the course of their choice in any state across India. The trust covers 100% of the tuition fees.

Students only need to pay for accommodation and examination charges, which are nominal.

Admission does not require any scholarship examination. Students should stay cautious of fake scholarship tests.

All admissions are provided only in government-approved institutions recognized by AICTE, PCI, NCI, NCBT, NBA, or NAAC.

Interested students can apply through the official website: www.unnatitrust.in

Our Mission

To ensure that no deserving student is deprived of higher education due to financial constraints, and to build an educated and self-reliant society.

Our Vision

To create an educated India where every student gets equal opportunities to learn, grow, and succeed regardless of their background.

What We Do

  • 100% tuition fee support for deserving students
  • Guidance for course and college selection
  • Direct admission assistance in government-approved institutions
  • Support for students from India and Nepal
  • Career counselling and academic guidance
