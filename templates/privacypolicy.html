Privacy Policy

By accessing and using our website, you agree to the terms of this Privacy Policy. Unnati Educational and Charitable Trust respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information. This policy explains how we collect, use, store, and safeguard the information you provide while using our website and services.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information from users:

  • Personal information such as your name, address, contact number, and email ID.
  • Details provided through online forms, chat, or email communication.
  • Educational and admission-related information submitted by you.
  • Any other information you voluntarily provide through our website.

2. Technical and Automatic Data

When you visit our website, certain information may be collected automatically to improve the user experience and website performance. This may include:

  • Your IP address.
  • Browser type and operating system.
  • Date, time, and location of access.
  • Pages visited and time spent on the website.

3. Use of Cookies

Our website may use cookies to enhance user experience. Cookies are small files stored on your device that help us understand user behavior and improve our services. You may choose to disable cookies through your browser settings if you prefer.

4. How We Use Your Information

The information collected may be used for the following purposes:

  • Processing student applications and inquiries.
  • Providing admission and scholarship-related services.
  • Communicating important updates and notifications.
  • Improving our website and services.
  • Maintaining internal records and compliance.

5. Information Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties. Your data may only be shared with authorized institutions, colleges, or regulatory bodies when required for admission or legal purposes.

6. Data Security

We take reasonable technical and organizational precautions to protect your personal data from unauthorized access, misuse, or disclosure. However, no online system is completely secure, and users are advised to take appropriate precautions while sharing personal information online.

7. Third-Party Links

Our website may contain links to third-party websites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices or content of those external sites.

8. Changes to This Policy

The trust reserves the right to update or modify this Privacy Policy at any time. Any changes will be posted on this page with the updated effective date.

9. Contact Us

If you have any questions regarding this Privacy Policy, you may contact us through the details provided on our website.
